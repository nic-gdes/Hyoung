Being a printer, MinutePress is able to offer many different types of prints, including:

  • Wallpaper
  • Brochures
  • Letterheads
  • Business cards

If you are interested in becoming an intern at MinutePress to learn more about the printing process, you must be able to meet their outlined criteria first:

  • Somewhat understand printing processes
  • File organization knowledge
  • Know types of files and importance
  • Nice if you have graphic design skills
  • Trustworthy, committed, good person
  • Do not cause drama
